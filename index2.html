Ni’am Tamami was born in Kediri, East Java, Indonesia in 1990. He received an S.ST. (Bachelor) degree in Applied Electronics Engineering from Politeknik Elektronika Negeri Surabaya (PENS) in 2012. He joined Politeknik Elektronika Negeri Surabaya (PENS) as lecturer in 2012. He received an M.T. (Master) degree in Applied Electronics Engineering from Politeknik Elektronika Negeri Surabaya (PENS) in 2014. He is now still active as D3 and D4 (3 and 4 years diploma) program lecturer, especially in Electrical Department. His teaching subjects are including Electronics, automation (see my Teaching Material).

Research Interest

Edit "Research Interest"
His Research Focus is about implementation of control system, robotics, computer vision.

 

Currently developed research

  1. Localization and Mapping for Autonomous VTOL

2. Control System and Image Processing for Agribot (Three axis linear robot for farming).

3. Localization and Mapping for AGV Robot based Robot Operating System

4. Smart weather station or disaster application based multi node sensor

5. Smart Fish Feeder

6. Smart Aquarium Controller

7. Control System and Robotic Implementation

8. Data Acquisition Application (Internet of Things, Radio based)
